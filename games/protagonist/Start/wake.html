Adventures of The Protagonist.

You open your eyes, and find that you were sleeping
on the wooden floorboard of a house. You stand up and see
a door in front of you, as well as a staircase. There is also
a hallway leading to a backdoor and garage behind you. You
rack your brain, trying to remember anything you can about
this abnormally ordinary house, trying to remember anything
about yourself or who you are. But the writers are lazy, and
wrote you no backstory. No personality, no traits, no appearance,
no purpose, nothing. There is nothing. You are the unnamed
protagonist.

A) Go out the front door.

B) Climb upstairs.

C) Enter the garage.

D) Go out the back door.
