Adventures of The Protagonist.

You decide to fight the zombies. You pick up an old
baseball bat and lift it over your shoulder. When the
first zombie approaches, you swing the bat and knock
him unconscious. Zombies are slow, so you find it rather
easy to clear out the yard. As go back inside and start
fortifying the house, you realise your purpose. The meaning
of existence is to keep existing, to survive at all
costs. You loot a few abandoned neighboring houses for
food, and plan on surviving as long as you can.
