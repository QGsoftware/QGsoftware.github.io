Adventures of The Protagonist.

You decide to escape the zombies. Zombies are slow,
and they can't be very hard to outrun. Besides, you don't
want to risk getting hurt in combat. You run past them,
and climb over the fence. You notice how all the houses
look abandoned, but not in ruins. The zombie outbreak
must have happened recently. You run until the road ends
at a forest. Just as you think you're safe, you step on
loose ground and fall into a ditch. The walls are too
steep to climb up, so you're stuck until the zombies
find you. You're toast.

GAME OVER
