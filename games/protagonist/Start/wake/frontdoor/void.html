Adventures of The Protagonist.

Theres no way you're spending the rest of your life
in this meaningless house. There would be no reason
to do anything, no purpose. The curiosity gets the
better of you, and you want so see how "infinite"
this world really is. Gravity feels normal, so that
must mean SOMETHING is pulling you down, right? You
decide to find out. You take a deep breath, count to
three, and jump. You look up as you see the bottom of
your house getting smaller and smaller until it's gone.
There is nothing. Nothing except you, and you question
how sure you are that you aren't nothing either. You fall
for hours, weeks, years, decades. You begin to lose
track of time. You keep thinking of all the things you
should have done before you fell into the void, but it's
too late now. You can't starve or die but you're basically
dead anyway. There is nothing but the void. The void is
everything. The void is meaningless. You are the void.
You are meaningless.
