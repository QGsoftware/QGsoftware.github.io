Adventures of The Protagonist.

You sit down on the floor of the garage. Why can't the writers
just give you a story? Why do they insist on watching you suffer?
If the writers won't give you a story, why should you be their
protagonist? You aren't going to take this anymore! If the writers
want you to be their protagonist, too bad! You're on strike
now! You lay on your back and stare at the ceiling. You refuse
to do anything until the writers give you a story. You lay there
and wait. Thousands, millions, maybe billions of years. But
you won't give in! You can't! You're on strike!

YOU GOT THE STRIKE ENDING!
