Adventures of The Protagonist.

You walk up the stairs and they squeak as you step. When you
reach the top, you see a door that is open at the end of the hall.
You walk to the room, and push the door open. You see a window,
a bed, and an open laptop running Quake Live. Is this your room? Could
the writers have felt bad enough about your situation that they
wrote in a room just for you? Perhaps they haven't forgotten about
you, perhaps you do indeed have a purpose! No... it can't be. If
it was your room, wouldn't you remember that you have a room? This
must be someone else's room. Perhaps you should rest, and ponder
things over.
