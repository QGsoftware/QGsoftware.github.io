Adventures of The Protagonist.

The tractor beam lifts you off the ground, and you decide
not to fight it. Maybe this is the writers leading you to
your purpose. You glide gracefully out of your window,
through the air, and into the disc shaped spaceship. The
hatch closes behind you, and you feel the weight of
gravity gradually increasing until it seems back to normal.
You look around at the room in front of you, it's a
bland room, square and made up of white tiles, each tile
about a meter long, and the room about 10 tiles across.
The room is completely empty except for a camera on the
farthest wall, but upon further inspection, you gasp and
realise it is partially biological. You quickly step back
from the eye, but you can't help staring at it. It just
looks so... alien. Figures, as you are on what looks to
be an alien ship. The half tech, half eyeball orb of
abomination turns to stare at you. The mess of technology
and biology is disgusting, yet so facinating. The eye
was a bit bigger than a softball, The pupil a dark red,
and the iris neon blue. The rest of the eye that was
supposed to be plain white, was pitch black. Various
technological bits and pieces surrounded the eye, and
held it in like a socket. You can almost feel it
staring into your soul. The sound of a door opening
distracts you from the strange, alien tech. You glance
behind you and see that a panel has opened up. A mysterious
figure slowly walks out, lifts some kind of weapon,
and takes a shot.

B) Look around for something to help you.
